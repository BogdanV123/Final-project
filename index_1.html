Main statements

For Honor - computer game, fighting game with a third person. Released by Ubisoft for PC, PlayStation 4 and Xbox One. In For Honor, players can control various forms of historical soldiers and warriors from various factions, namely Vikings,Wy lin, knights and samurai. The game takes place in a medieval setting.
